Crispy Chilli Babycorn

steps

ingredients
  • firstly, in a large vessel boil 3 cup water.
  • add in 9 chopped baby corn and simmer for 5 minutes.
  • drain off the baby corn and keep aside.
  • further, in a large mixing bowl take ½ cup maida and ¼ cup corn flour.
  • also add ½ tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp pepper, ½ tsp ginger paste and ½ tsp salt.
  • add in ½ cup water and prepare a smooth lump free batter.
  • furthermore, add in blanched baby corn and coat well.
  • deep fry in hot oil or preheat and bake at 180 degree celcius for 30 minutes.
  • stir occasionally and fry until the baby corn turns golden and crisp.
  • finally, drain off the fried baby corn over a kitchen towel to remove excess oil. keep aside.
  • firstly, in a large kadai heat 3 tsp oil and saute 2 green chilli, 1 inch ginger and 3 clove on high flame.
  • now add ½ onion, ½ capsicum and saute on high flame
  • additionally add 2 tbsp tomato sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar, 1 tbsp soy sauce, ¼ tsp pepper, ¼ tsp sugar and ¼ tsp salt.
  • mix well and saute for a minute.
  • add cornflour slurry. to prepare corn flour water mix 1 tsp of corn flour with ¼ cup of water.
  • additionally, add fried baby corn and 2 tbsp spring onion
  • mix gently making sure the sauce has coated well uniformly
  • finally, transfer chilli baby corn to a serving bowl and garnish with chopped spring onions green.
  1. baby corn (chopped)
  2. plain flour
  3. corn flour
  4. kashmiri red chilli powder
  5. pepper (crushed)
  6. ginger paste
  7. salt
  8. oil for frying
  9. green chilli (slit)
  10. ginger (finely chopped)
  11. clove (finely chopped)
  12. spring onion (finely chopped)
  13. capsicum (cubed)
  14. tomato sauce
  15. vinegar
  16. soy sauce
