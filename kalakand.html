Kalakand

steps

ingredients
  • Mash paneer coarsely and mix in the Nestlé MILKMAID and Nestlé Everyday Dairy Whitener.
  • Heat the mixture in a thick bottom pan
  • Cook on medium flame with constant stirring until the mixture becomes thick and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  • Remove from the flame and spread onto a greased plate. Sprinkle elaichi powder.
  • Cool and cut into squares.
  1. Nestlé MILKMAID
  2. Nestlé Everyday Dairy Whitener
  3. Paneer
  4. Elaichi (Cardamom) Powder
