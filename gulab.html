Gulab jamun

steps

ingredients
  • Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire
  • add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  • In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé Milkmaid, baking powder and baking soda
  • Mix gently to make a soft dough, do not knead too much.
  • Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns.
  • Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color.
  • Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup
  • once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame.
  • Serve warm.
  1. Nestlé Milkmaid
  2. Flour
  3. Paneer
  4. Sooji/Rava
  5. Baking Powder
  6. Baking Soda
  7. Oil
  8. Water
  9. Sugar
  10. Cardamom
